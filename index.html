Karkuma Turmeric Immune Booster is a functional food that helps maintain a strong immune system and supple health.
Karkuma Immune Plus is a functional food, designed to help the immune system and liver function better.
Karkuma Joint Guard is a functional food designed for anyone who has joint pain, joint stress or experiences joint stiffness, and it may help repair cartilage.
Karkuma Healthy Gut is formulated to keep your gut health & digestive functions working smoothly, efficiently, and optimally.
